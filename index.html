About

I am Denis Lafontant an experienced Full-Stack Web Designer and Developer. After 15 years working for the Haitian National Police of Haiti where I inhabited multiple roles, I have dedicated myself to computer sciences especially web development, enterprise solutions, and networking

I decided to leave to pursue my passion (coding) and learn every aspect needed to be a great programmer. I attended Microverse, an Online Coding School, which have me mastered all the hard skills (HTML, CSS, Ruby, SQL, Ruby on Rails, React & Redux, Javascript) and the soft skills (Pair Programming, Mentoring, Communication, Remote Working, Conflict Resolution, Team Collaboration).

Now I can implement business solutions by transforming the needs and problems into code, giving valuable advice to help the business grow with technology, I also do digital marketing to promote and grow your business on social networks.

All of the projects that I work on are guided by a strong sense of meticulousness and precision both design and development. I create meaningful and unique digital experience for my client's users.
